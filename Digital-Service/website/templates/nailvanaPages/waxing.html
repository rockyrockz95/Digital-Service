Waxing 😎

Waxing is another popular service offered at spas. It involves applying a layer of warm wax to the skin, which adheres to the hair. When the wax is quickly pulled off, it removes the hair from the root, leaving the skin smooth and hair-free. Waxing can be done on various parts of the body, including the legs, arms, underarms, and bikini area. It provides longer-lasting results compared to shaving, as it takes longer for the hair to grow back. Additionally, frequent waxing can lead to finer and sparser hair regrowth over time.
